Jason Das

Pax Tecum Filomena

“Pax Tecum Filomena” is a notated musical composition by Jason Das, scored for oboe, viola, and guitar, written 2020-2021.

As of late February, 2021, the piece is in a late-draft stage. It will surely be revised and refined further in consultation with human musicians.
