Pax Tecum Filomena

“Pax Tecum Filomena” is a notated musical composition by Jason Das, scored for oboe, viola, and guitar, written 2020-2021.

As of February, 2021, the piece is in a late-draft stage. It will surely be revised and refined further in consultation with human musicians.

The piece is in five movements, preceded by an invocation:

Invocation
  condensation layer
I. Upon Arrival
  along a path
II. Late Afternoon, Late Summer
  meadow moths
  an unexpected visitor
III. Filomena From the Hills
  juvenile delinquent
IV. Sweet and Bitter Plums
V. Night
  towards the sound of the breakers
  moonlight emerges from clouds
