Wintercolors

Original paintings of winter scenes by Jason Das.

Made with watercolor and ink on 140lb cold-press Fabriano paper. Each one is unique. These are not prints. They are original paintings. Great as gifts or for yourself!

Conveniently sized for standard US picture frames at 4" x 6" and 5" x 7".

4" x 6" paintings are $35. 5" x 7" paintings are $45.

Want one? (Or more than one?) E-mail jason@jasondas.com to order. It will be the cost of the painting(s) plus $7 for USPS Domestic Priority shipping for as many as you want. (If you want a different shipping method or you’re international, no problem; we’ll figure it out.)

Payment is easy via PayPal, Venmo, etc. (I wasted a few hours failing to set up a “real” online store for these and it was a wormhole of nonsense. So let’s just do it the old-fashioned way. If you want some of my zines, too, we can combine those in.)
